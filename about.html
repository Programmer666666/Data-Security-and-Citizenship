About page

This is the about page where you will learn all about us!




What we do

We strive to give out as much data security/citizenship information as possible to help students and give them a better learning experience.


What we aim to do

  • Please students with the information given knowing that they will leave looking for what they came for.
  • Update our website if anything new appears to keep everyone always up to date.
  • Spread the awareness of how important cyber security is and how serious it should be taken so less malicious things happen to people.

The website

We have multiple pages for you to look through on our website.

Here is an idea of what you will find on these pages.

  • Homepage – welcoming you to the site and recommending pages to you.
  • Data security – everything you need to know about data security and why It is so important.
  • Data growth – the growth of data over time and the info on it.
  • Data factors – all the best information of data factors is on this page. Data factors is a very interesting topic so go check the page out now!
  • About us – the page you’re on right now.
  • Contact – this is where you will find info on us such as phone number, email, etc.
  • Gallery – this is where you will find images about the topics on our website, we hope these images somewhat inspire you.
  • Legislation – the overall laws on data security.

Why we started this website

We started this website because it was given to us as a task by a lecturer to help students that need information or just needed to know. And we are aiming to live up to that expectation and give every student the best information on these topics.
