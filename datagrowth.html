About Data Growth

Learn what Data Citizenship means, how you can find data growth and how you can use it. Data is growing as an industry in many areas including technology, economics, and society. The areas of data growth can be accounted for due to the many ways big data sets can be used to analyse previous trends and predict future trends. If you look for a way to help any area in life, the analysis of a data set is a great place to start.




Why data is helpful in a business

We see that data helps grow through making accurate decisions on the understanding of customers interests and demands. When you have a website or social media platform that promotes your business, you are collecting data from viewers/readers who visit your promoted area of business. With businesses we make decisions for the company via, global news, your own personal instincts, and the current presence off of social media.
Data is helping us solve problems and track general performance on daily actions, whether that be for government purposes, societal and or technologically. We experience slow and fast performances throughout all areas of data, this is part of data growth. Its challenging to collect, analyse, process, and conclude many data types. However, doing those steps in a business of whatever kind, will help make it better than before.
